# LinkedIn Carousel Specifications

## Dimensions

| Format | Dimensions | Use Case |
|--------|------------|----------|
| **Square** | 1080×1080 px | Standard, works on all devices (recommended) |
| **Vertical** | 1080×1350 px | More screen real estate on mobile |

## Safe Zone

- **960×960 px** centered area is guaranteed visible
- Content outside safe zone may be cropped on some devices
- Keep critical text/CTAs within safe zone

## Limits

- **Minimum slides**: 2
- **Maximum slides**: 20
- **File format**: PDF (multi-page)
- **Max file size**: 100 MB

## Content Guidelines

- **Headlines**: Max 120 characters, visible at a glance
- **Body text**: Keep under 480 characters per slide
- **Font size**: Minimum 24pt for readability on mobile
- **Contrast**: WCAG AA compliant (4.5:1 ratio minimum)

## Visual Design Principles

- **Consistent branding** across all slides
- **Bold, scannable** text - people swipe quickly
- **One idea per slide** - don't overcrowd
- **Use white space** - let content breathe
- **Brand colors** in header/footer for recognition

## Engagement Tips

- Start with a question or bold statement
- Use numbered lists ("5 Ways to...", "3 Mistakes...")
- Include your photo/logo on first and last slides
- End with a clear CTA (Follow, Comment, Share, Link in bio)

## Slide Structure

1. **Hook Slide** (Slide 1) - Compelling title that makes people swipe
2. **Content Slides** (Slides 2-N) - Value-packed information
3. **CTA Slide** (Final) - Clear call to action
